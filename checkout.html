What is a SD$, SD$ Coin, or simply, $$?

The SD$ is a proxy currency to be used for site based transactions, to primarily insure fraud prevention and complete member privacy. Because you are not dealing in real currency, we are not obliged to report transaction to the appropriate agency. We leave that entirely up to you and your sound judgement.

What does it cost?

It can then be gifted directly - account to account, or be used to purchase anything from our online marketplace - which incedentally happens to be a store with the wworlds largest catalog of products.
